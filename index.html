Problem

You've just bought a print of some amazing art, and you want to put it up on the wall. You can look round the hardware store but the frames are all the wrong size. What you need is a mounting board for the image.

Measure

To size this up, take the frame apart and measure the height and width in millimeters. Measure the height and width of the print too. You can adjust the sizes and colour to find something that will look pleasing to the eye. There's a zoom function if you find your image getting too big.

Size

This lets you work out the suitable size for a mounting board for your picture.

Mount

Finally, tape the print into the mount, then assumble it into the fram and hang on your wall.
